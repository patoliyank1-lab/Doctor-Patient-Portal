##  public page
- Home page 
- about us page
- doctors page - list of verified doctors with filtering options.
- doctor profile page - detailed information about a specific doctor.
- About us page.

- registration page - for new patients and doctors to create an account.


## Patient portal
- Dashboard page:
    - upcoming appointments
    - past appointments
    - notifications
- find doctor page:
    - search for doctors 
    - view doctor profiles 
    - appointment booking page
- appointment management page:
    - view and manage upcoming appointments
    - cancel appointments
- medical records page:
    - view and manage medical records
    - upload new medical records
- My Reviews page:
    - view and manage reviews for doctors
    - write new reviews for doctors
- Notifications page:
    - view and manage notifications related to appointments, messages, and system updates
- Profile management page:
    - view and update personal information, medical history, and preferences

## Doctor portal
- Dashboard page:
    - today's appointments
    - pending appointments
    - doctor's profile information
    - notifications
- Appointment management page:
    - view and manage upcoming appointments
    - accept or reject appointment requests
    - reschedule appointments
- Availability management page:
    - set and update available time slots for appointments
    - block off unavailable times
- notifications page:
    - view and manage notifications related to appointments, messages, and system updates
- Profile management page:
    - view and update personal information, qualifications, and experience

## Admin portal
- Dashboard page:
    - overview of system statistics (number of doctors, patients, appointments, etc.)
    - recent activities and notifications
    - graphs and charts for data visualization
- Doctor management page:
    - view and manage doctor profiles
    - verify doctor credentials
    - approve or reject doctor registrations
- Patient management page:
    - view and manage patient profiles
    - block or unblock patient accounts
- Appointment management page:
    - view and manage all appointments in the system
- Audit Logs page:
    - view and manage system audit logs for security and compliance purposes



## Functionalities

# public access
- Home page: A welcoming landing page that provides an overview of the platform's services and features.
- about us page: Information about the platform, its mission, vision, and team members.
- Doctors page: A directory of verified doctors with filtering options based on specialization, location, and availability.
- Doctor profile page: Detailed information about a specific doctor, including qualifications, experience, patient reviews, and contact information.
